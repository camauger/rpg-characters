About Our Site

Welcome to our RPG Character Showcase!

Our site is dedicated to bringing to life a diverse array of characters from the realm of Role-Playing Games (RPGs). We believe that every character has a unique story to tell, and we're here to share these stories with you.

Our Characters

Our characters are more than just avatars in a game. They are carefully crafted entities with distinct attributes such as age, gender, ethnicity, class, subclass, and background. Each character is unique, with its own personality and backstory.

Whether you're looking for inspiration for your next RPG campaign, or you simply enjoy exploring the rich tapestry of characters that the RPG world has to offer, we're sure you'll find something to pique your interest here.

Our Process

Our characters are generated using a sophisticated Python script that combines randomness with a set of predefined rules to create diverse and interesting characters. The script also manages the characters, storing them in a JSON file, and even supports image optimization and Discord bot integration.

Explore and Enjoy

While our current site functionality is focused on displaying characters, we have big plans for the future. We're working on adding features that will allow you to interact with the characters in new and exciting ways.

In the meantime, we invite you to explore our collection of characters. Dive into their stories, imagine their adventures, and let them inspire your own RPG journeys.

Thank you for visiting our site. We hope you enjoy your stay in our world of characters!
