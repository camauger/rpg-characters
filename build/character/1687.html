Findlay Isusmoral

Dwarf Male Criminal

Rogue - Assassin

Background Story

<br>Findlay Isusmoral was born into a criminal family in the Forgotten Realms. His father was a thief and his mother was a prostitute. He grew up on the streets, learning how to pick pockets and fight. When he was ten, his parents were killed in a robbery gone wrong. Findlay was left to fend for himself. He turned to a life of crime to survive, using his skills to pick pockets and steal. He quickly learned that he was good at it and started to enjoy the thrill of the chase. He started to take bigger risks, and his crimes began to escalate. He was eventually caught and sent to prison. In prison, he met a man who introduced him to the world of rogues. He taught Findlay how to be a better thief and how to use his skills to make a living. When he was released from prison, Findlay decided to become a professional thief.
