Background Story

Isador Lymi was born to a family of scholars in the city of Waterdeep. From a young age, he showed a great aptitude for learning and was always asking questions. His parents encouraged his love of knowledge, and he spent many hours in the library, poring over books on history, geography, and anything else he could find. When he was sixteen, Isador left home to travel the world and see all that he could. He had many adventures, and made new friends (and enemies) along the way. He also discovered his talent for thievery, and quickly learned how to pick locks and pockets. Eventually, Isador settled down in the city of Baldur's Gate, where he opened a bookstore.
