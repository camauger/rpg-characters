Background Story

Baxy Lorloriuskal was born in a small village in the Forgotten Realms, far from the hustle and bustle of the larger cities. His parents were both outlanders, but his mother had a hint of Aasimar heritage in her blood. Growing up, Baxy was always a bit of an outsider, but he was also a natural leader. He was always the first to volunteer for any task, and he was always eager to help his neighbors. As Baxy grew older, he began to feel a strong pull towards adventure. He wanted to explore the world and see what it had to offer. He decided to leave his village and become a fighter, traveling the land and helping those in need. He quickly gained a reputation as a brave and noble warrior, and he was often called upon to help protect the innocent from the forces of evil. Baxy's travels eventually led him to a mysterious temple deep in the forest. He had heard rumors of a powerful artifact hidden within the temple, and he was determined to find it. He was sure that the artifact could help him in his quest to protect the innocent, and he was determined to uncover its secrets. He hopes that by unlocking the mysteries of the temple, he can finally fulfill his destiny and become the hero he was always meant to be.

Baxy Lorloriuskal is an independent-minded individual who values his freedom above all else. He is wise and brave, but can be cowardly when faced with difficult situations.
