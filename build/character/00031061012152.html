Peta Isana

Elf Female Acolyte

Monk - Way of the Open Hand

Background Story

Peta Isana was born in the small elven village of Lirith, nestled in the heart of the High Forest. Her parents were devout followers of the goddess of the moon, Selune, and raised her to be a devout acolyte. Peta was a bright and curious child, and her parents encouraged her to explore the world around her. She spent her days learning the ways of the forest, and her nights studying the teachings of Selune. As Peta grew older, she began to feel a strong connection to the divine. She was drawn to the teachings of the monks, and soon began to train in the martial arts of the monastery. Peta quickly excelled in her studies, and her teachers were impressed by her dedication and skill. Peta's life changed when she was chosen to join a group of adventurers on a quest to find a powerful artifact. The artifact was said to be hidden in a long-forgotten temple deep in the High Forest. Peta was eager to prove her worth, and she threw herself into the quest with enthusiasm. After a long and dangerous journey, the adventurers were successful in their mission. Now, Peta Isana is a respected monk, and she continues to seek out new challenges. She is driven by a desire to prove her worth and to bring glory to Selune. Peta is also determined to uncover the secrets of the forgotten temple, and she hopes to one day uncover the truth behind the powerful artifact she helped to find.

Personality

Peta Isana is a selfless and resourceful individual who is driven by knowledge. She is an optimist at heart, but her cynical nature can sometimes get in the way of her goals. She is always looking for ways to help others and is willing to go the extra mile to make sure her actions are beneficial to those around her.
