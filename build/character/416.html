Maxie Mir

Tiefling Male Mercenary Veteran

Bard - College of Glamour

Background Story

Maxie Mir was born into a family of mercenaries. His father was a well-known mercenary and his mother was a bard. Maxie grew up hearing stories of his father's exploits and his mother's songs. When he was old enough, he joined his father's mercenary company. He quickly proved himself to be a skilled warrior and an asset to the company. After a few years, Maxie left the company to strike out on his own. He quickly became known as one of the best mercenaries in the realm. He was hired by kings and queens, and even the occasional dragon. He always completed his contracts, no matter how dangerous they were. Eventually, Maxie retired from mercenary work. He settled down in a small town and took up his mother's profession of bard. He would often regale the townsfolk with stories of his adventures. They would listen, wide-eyed, as he told of his battles and triumphs. Max

Personality

Maxie Mir is an idealistic and courageous person. He is also reckless and impatient, and has a strong protective instinct. He is playful and good-natured.
