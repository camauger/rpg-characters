Lindie Irhi

Dwarf Female Archaeologist

Barbarian - Path of the Berserker

Background Story

Lindie Irhi was born to a small tribe of barbarians in the Forgotten Realms. She was always adventurous, exploring the wilderness and looking for new places to go. When she was sixteen, she left her tribe to find her own way in the world. She quickly realized that she loved adventure and archaeology. She began to explore ancient ruins and tombs, looking for lost artifacts. She quickly became known as an expert in her field. She also began to realize that she could use her knowledge to manipulate people. She would often find ancient artifacts and then blackmail the people who wanted them. She became very successful, but also very unpopular. She didn't care, as long as she was able to have adventures and explore the world.

Personality

Lindie Irhi's ideal is adventurousness. Her flaw is manipulative, her bond is protection, her behavior is pessimistic, and her nature is adventurous.
