Colin Danyel

Elf Male Cloistered Scholar

Sorcerer - Draconic Bloodline

Background Story

Colin Danyel was once a promising young student at the Cloistered Scholar Sorcery academy. He was top of his class in all his studies and was on track to become one of the greats. However, his temper got the better of him one day and he was expelled from the academy. Colin was still determined to prove himself and become a great sorcerer. He began to study on his own, learning all he could about magic. He also began to experiment with his own spells, creating new and more powerful ones. Colin slowly began to make a name for himself in the magical community. However, he always felt like an outsider, like he didn't quite belong. This only made him more determined to be the best. Colin's ambition knows no bounds and he will stop at nothing to achieve his goals.

Personality

Colin Danyel's ideal is creativity. His flaw is hot-tempered, his bond is equality, his behavior is ambitious, and his nature is reserved.
