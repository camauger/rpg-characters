Background Story

Van Ellea was born into a family of knights. His father was a great knight who fought in many battles and his mother was a gentle woman who cared for the sick and needy. When Van Ellea was just a boy, his father was killed in battle and his mother died of a disease. Van Ellea was left alone in the world. He was taken in by a family of knights who raised him and taught him the ways of knighthood. When he was old enough, he joined the knights and fought in many battles. He became a great knight himself and was respected by all. However, Van Ellea always felt that something was missing in his life. He was not content with just being a knight. He wanted more.
