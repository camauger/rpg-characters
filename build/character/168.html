Fabien Fel

Half-Elf Male City Watch

Rogue - Thief

Background Story

Fabien Fel grew up on the streets of Athkatla, the capital of Amn. He was always a small child, and was picked on because of it. When he was thirteen, he decided he'd had enough. He started to learn how to pick pockets and fight dirty. He quickly became the best in his gang. When he was sixteen, he tried to rob a nobleman. He was caught and brought before the City Watch. He expected to be executed, but instead was given a choice: join the City Watch or go to prison. He chose the City Watch. He proved to be a natural at being a rogue, and quickly rose through the ranks. He's now one of the City Watch's best. He's still small, but he's learned to use that to his advantage. He's also learned to control his temper, and is known for being calm in a crisis.

Personality

Fabien Fel is an idealistic person who believes in equality for all. He can be stubborn at times and has strong faith. He is introverted and resourceful.
