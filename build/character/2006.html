Albina Ser

Dwarf Female Hermit

Barbarian - Path of the Battlerager

Background Story

Albina Ser was born into a family of nomadic herders in the Forgotten Realms. When she was five years old, her parents were killed by a group of bandits. Albina and her younger brother, Aiden, were the only survivors. Aiden was taken in by a kindly farmer, but Albina ran away into the wilderness. She survived by her wits and her fists, becoming a fierce fighter. After a few years, she stumbled upon a hermit's cave. The hermit, a wise old man, took her in and taught her the ways of the world. Albina learned from him how to live off the land, and how to defend herself from those who would do her harm. She also learned that the best way to help others is to first help oneself.
