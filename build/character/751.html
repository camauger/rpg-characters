Merl A

Human Non-binary City Watch

Bard - College of Lore

Background Story

Merl A was born into a family of bards who traveled the Forgotten Realms performing for anyone who would listen. When Merl A was old enough, they joined their family on the road. They quickly realized that they didn't enjoy the nomadic lifestyle and yearned for a place to call home. They eventually settled in a small town and joined the city watch. Merl A uses their bardic talents to keep the peace and help the citizens of the town. They are always looking for ways to help others and make the world a better place.

Personality

Merl A's ideal is freedom. Merl A's traits are: easily influenced, faith, timid and resourceful.
