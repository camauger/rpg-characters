Thadeus In

Half-Elf Male Sage

Cleric - Nature Domain

Background Story

<br>Thadeus In was born into a small farming community in the Forgotten Realms. He was raised believing that all creatures could live in harmony if they just tried hard enough. When Thadeus was old enough, he left home to explore the world and learn more about the different races that lived within it. He quickly realized that not everyone shared his ideal of harmony. He saw the war and violence that plagued the world and became pessimistic about the chances of ever achieving true peace. Thadeus still believes in his ideal, but he has become rebellious in his behavior, refusing to stand by and do nothing while the world around him is in turmoil. His loyalty lies with those who share his ideal, and he will do whatever he can to help them achieve their goals.

Personality

<br>Thadeus In is an idealistic person who believes in harmony. However, he is also a pessimist who often sees the negative side of things. He is very loyal to his community and often behaves in a rebellious manner.
