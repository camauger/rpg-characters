Junette Riktorara

Gnome Female Faction Agent

Fighter - Psi Warrior

Background Story

Junette Riktorara was born to a small family of farmers in the Forgotten Realms. When she was five, her parents were killed by a band of orcs who raided her village. She was the only survivor. Junette was taken in by the local lord and raised by his family. When she was sixteen, she enlisted in the lord's army and fought against the orcs who had destroyed her village. After the war, she became a Faction Agent Fighter. She has dedicated her life to protecting others from the same fate she suffered as a child.

Personality

Junette Riktorara is an idealistic person who values wisdom above all else. However, she is also cowardly and prone to backing down from a fight or confrontation. Her strong sense of forgiveness means that she is always willing to give people a second chance, even if they don't deserve it. Junette's aggressive behavior can be off-putting to some, but those who know her well know that it is simply her nature to be bold and outspoken.
