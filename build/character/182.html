Background Story

Lian Elle is a cautious and timid Elf who has dedicated her life to discovery and knowledge. She has spent many years travelling the world in search of ancient artifacts and ruins. Her travels have led her to the Forgotten Realms, where she has become a renowned Archaeologist. Lian is a skilled Windwalker, and uses her abilities to navigate the dangerous ruins she explores. She is often manipulative, using her knowledge to convince others to help her in her quest for discovery.
