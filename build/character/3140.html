Dall Aniaes

Elf Male Urchin

Ranger - Beast Master

Background Story


Dall was born on the streets of Waterdeep and has spent most of his life scrambling to get by. He's always had a quick wit and a sharp tongue, which has served him well in getting out of tight situations. He's also always had a soft spot for nature and the creatures that live in it. When he was younger, he would often sneak into the gardens of the nobility to catch a glimpse of the beauty within.
As he grew older, Dall realized that the world was not as fair as he had thought. He saw the inequality between the rich and the poor and the way that those in power abused their station. He was no longer content to sit by and do nothing, so he took up the mantle of the ranger. Now he uses his skills to fight for the oppressed and to even the score. He still finds time to appreciate the natural world, but now he also knows that there is darkness lurking within it.

Personality


Dall Aniaes is an idealistic person who believes in equality for all. He can be impatient at times, but he is generally optimistic and honest. He has a strong connection to nature and enjoys spending time outdoors.
