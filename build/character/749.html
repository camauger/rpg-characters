Cob Galmondisdor

Human Male Noble

Druid - Circle of the Shepherd

Background Story

Cob Galmondisdor was born into a wealthy family of druids who resided in the forest of Cormanthor. He was raised with the belief that all creatures are equal and should be treated as such. When he came of age, he left his home to explore the world and learn more about the different races and cultures that inhabited it. He quickly realized that not everyone shared his views on equality and began to fight for the rights of all creatures, no matter what their race or creed. He quickly gained a reputation as a fierce warrior and champion of the oppressed. He has since settled down in the city of Waterdeep, where he continues to fight for the rights of all creatures and promote equality among all.

Personality

Cob Galmondisdor's ideal is innovation. His flaw is prideful, his bond is equality, his behavior is aggressive, and his nature is inquisitive.
