Tatiania Iusaldnyxer

Gnome Female Charlatan

Sorcerer - Divine Soul

Background Story

Tatiania Iusaldnyxer was born into a noble family in the city of Neverwinter. From a young age, she showed an aptitude for magic and was sent to study at the city's prestigious academy. However, Tatiania quickly grew disillusioned with the academy's rigid structure and traditional ways of teaching. She began to experiment with her magic, seeking to find new and more powerful ways to use it. This led her down a dangerous path, and she soon began to dabble in the dark arts. Her experiments attracted the attention of the city's guards, and she was forced to flee Neverwinter. She eventually made her way to the city of Waterdeep, where she began to ply her trade as a charlatan sorcerer. Tatiania is a skilled con artist and manipulator. She uses her magic to deceive and trick people, always seeking to gain an advantage for herself. She is a master of illusion and deception

Personality

Tatiania Iusaldnyxer is a wise and kind person who is very growth-oriented. However, she can be untrustworthy at times, and she is very attached to tradition.
