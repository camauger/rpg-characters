Background Story

Rick was born to a family of farmers in a small village in the middle of nowhere. When Rick was five, his parents were killed by a band of raiders who burned down his village. Rick was the only survivor. He was found by a group of traveling mercenaries who took him in and raised him as one of their own. Rick learned how to fight and how to survive in the wild. When Rick was sixteen, the mercenaries were hired by a local lord to help put down a rebellion. The lord was a cruel man who ordered his men to slaughter the rebels, even though they were unarmed. Rick refused to participate in the massacre and was kicked out of the mercenaries. With nowhere else to go, Rick decided to join the local guild of artisans. He quickly learned how to make and repair things. He also learned how to use magic, and he discovered that he had a natural talent for it. He eventually rose to the rank of Guild Artisan.
