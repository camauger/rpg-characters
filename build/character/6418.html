Martinsen Yndor

Half-Orc Non-binary Cloistered Scholar

Wizard - School of Conjuration

Background Story

Martinsen Yndor was born into a minor noble family in Waterdeep. As the youngest child, Martinsen was often left to their own devices and spent a lot of time reading. When they were old enough, they left Waterdeep to study at the Cloistered Scholar Wizard academy in Candlekeep.
Martinsen excelled at their studies, and quickly became one of the top students in their class. However, they also began to develop a manipulative streak. They would often use their knowledge to take advantage of others, both inside and outside of the academy.
As Martinsen's studies came to an end, they began to grow restless. They chafed at the strict rules of the academy and longed to see the world. Martinsen finally left the academy and struck out on their own.
Now, Martinsen travels the world, using their knowledge to help (or hinder) those they meet. They always have their eye on the mysteries of the arcane, seeking to unearth the secrets of magic for personal growth, sometimes even at the cost of others.
During their journey, Martinsen discovered a mysterious artifact, a conjuring orb, believed to summon a beast of immense power from another realm. The artifact, however, is missing a crucial gemstone to function. The search for this missing piece, believed to be hidden in the dangerous caves of the Underdark, now drives Martinsen's quest, promising not only a thrilling adventure but also a potential for unimaginable power - a temptation that could test their ideal of selflessness.

Personality


Martinsen Yndor is a manipulative and rebellious person who is honest and believes in selflessness.
