Allyce Is

Dragonborn Female Archaeologist

Rogue - Thief

Background Story

Allyce Is was born into a family of archaeologists in the Forgotten Realms. She grew up listening to stories of ancient civilizations and dreaming of discovering lost treasures. When she was old enough, she set out on her own to explore the world. She quickly realized that she had a talent for finding hidden objects and unlocking ancient mysteries. She also discovered that she enjoyed the thrill of adventure and the excitement of combat. She became a rogue archaeologist, using her skills to find treasure and fight monsters. She has made a name for herself as a daring adventurer, and she is always looking for her next big adventure.

Personality

Allyce Is is an idealist who longs for unity in the world. She can be vengeful when she feels betrayed, and she is fiercely loyal to her friends and family. Allyce is an intuitive thinker and an adventurous spirit.
