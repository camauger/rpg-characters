Background Story

Tann Calvivyn was born to a family of scholars in the Forgotten Realms. As a child, he showed great promise in his studies and was quickly accepted into the prestigious Cloistered Scholars. Tann excelled in his studies, but always felt a little out of place among his peers. He was always more interested in the physical world and the creatures that inhabited it. This led Tann to take up the study of barbarianism. He immersed himself in the study of the wild and the savage, and quickly became one of the most knowledgeable scholars on the subject. This knowledge came in handy when his family was attacked by a group of orcs. Tann fought alongside the barbarians and helped drive the orcs back. Since then, Tann has continued to study both the scholarly and the barbaric. He has come to see the value in both, and believes that the two can coexist in harmony.
