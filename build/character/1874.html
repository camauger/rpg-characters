Shay Rayn

Tiefling Male Gladiator

Bard - College of Whispers

Background Story

Shay Rayn was born into a family of gladiators. His father was a well-known gladiator and his mother was a bard. Shay's parents didn't have much, but they made sure their children had everything they needed. Shay's older brother, Denton, was always getting into trouble. Shay would often try to help him, but Denton would never listen. When Shay was old enough, he joined his father in the arena. It was there that he found his true passion - performing. He would sing and dance while his father fought. The crowds loved him and he quickly became a sensation. Shay loved the attention and the adoration of the crowds. He loved the feeling of being in the spotlight. However, his father was killed in one of his matches. Shay was devastated. He vowed to never perform again. Shay Rayn is an altruistic person who believes in putting others before himself. He can be short-tempered at times, but he is fiercely loyal to his family and friends. Shay has a positive outlook on life and is always up for a new adventure.
